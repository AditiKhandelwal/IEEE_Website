EVENTS

AARAMBH

AARAMBH is a social educational initiative undertaken by the students of IEEE, MANIT to encourage and support innovative and creative approaches to serving the needs of students in the underprivileged section of the society. IEEE believes that education is fundamental to an equitable society. Education equips children and youth with the knowledge, skills, values and mindsets needed to be empowered in individuals and responsible citizens. IEEE aims to eradicate the root cause of the education crisis in the deprived sections of the community and to bring forth an organic change in the society.

SRAJAN

SRAJAN is the student project contest, organized by IEEE student branch, MANIT Bhopal, with the tagline ‘Ideate || Innovate || Create’, the contest aims to encourage out of the box ideas and provide a platform to present creativity and innovation. The contest will look at innovation as a process focused on a range of disciplines in electronics, electrical and computer science engineering branches. The contest acts as an opportunity for the students to display their novel ideas & a chance to discuss and debate these ideas among people with similar intellectual abilities. It fosters innovation and applied learning among students.

SCEECS

IEEE International Students´ Conference on Electrical, Electronics and Computer Sciences is a biennial event. This flagship conference of IEEE M.P Sub Section features a comprehensive technical program including paper presentations, workshops and expert guest lectures.We aim to provide a platform for the brightest students from all over the world to present and share their ideas, discuss recent developments with likeminded peers and interact with eminent researchers and academicians. SCEECS is a multi- disciplinary conference covering a range of tracks and topics from Electrical, Electronics and Computer Sciences.
